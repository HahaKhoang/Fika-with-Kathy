What is fika?

Fika is a Swedish word that means to take a coffee/pastry break throughout the day. Fika is important to Swedish culture because it places importance on spending time socializing with friends/colleagues. This concept helps strengthen relationships with people you know. So let’s grab a cup of coffee together and learn to speak English over fika!
